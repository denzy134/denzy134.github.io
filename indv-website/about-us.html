LPG Cylinder storage information

Basis rules for LPG cylinder Storage

  • Cylinders should be used and stored in the upright position
  • Cylinders should be stored in well ventilated places away from sources of heat, ignition sources and readily ignitable materials
  • Cylinders must not be stored or used below ground level
  • Cylinders should preferably be stored outdoors, away from buildings entry/exit points and feature such as drain Cylinder must not be kept near to any corrosive, toxic or oxidant material.
